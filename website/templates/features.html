Our core goal here at Lensa is to create a platform that revolutionizes the way companies find talent. The basis of this is a competency model which covers all those knowledge, skills, abilities, attitudes that are the most important for companies from a wide variety of verticals, industries and markets. Beyond the knowledge of our behavioral scientists, this continuous process leverages a host of valuable input that we gather from HR professionals, fellow scientists and various market players.

Taking our efforts to the next level, we are now turning to the HR community as a whole, looking to get even more professional feedback through this open platform.
We are Lensa. The next game changer in recruitment. Our platform is set to optimize the way people find jobs and companies find talent. We take search out of job search and offer simple, easy to use services powered by smart technologies to make a better match between candidates and companies. On the surface, it's fun. Candidates play games so we can find them better jobs. We give companies intuitive services that match the best, most qualified candidates to their needs. Under the hood, we use complex machine learning algorithms to find the right combination of hard skills and soft skills that would make a person successful at a job. We also make sure it’s the right fit in terms of team and culture. We analyze resumes, get candidates to play serious games, and benchmark companies to make sure we find that right fit. We believe that the search is over. We know the best way to find a perfect job and a perfect candidate is not searching. It’s finding the perfect match.

Grab the key to efficiency with an intuitive feature set that assists fluent workflow including job posting, data organization and comparison, scheduling and communication.

Centralization

At the strategic core of Lensa, we have developed a robust data storage, search and mining capacity which retains all information from previous talent searches.

Never lose or miss a candidate – the most precious asset of recruiting.

Searchable candidate database

The extensive search capabilities of Lensa offer access to all previous recruitment data by keyword, experience, education, status, skills, and more. Search criteria sets can be saved and used again for convenience. The system meets data handling regulations.

Did we mention search component is incredibly fast?

Document storage

From interview questions to contract templates, any recruitment-related document can be uploaded into the system. Position profiles are automatically stored in PDF format.

Access your library any time, from anywhere.

Reports

The centralized database can be leveraged to generate reusable, customized, Excel compatible reports covering a wide range of information.

E-mail application

E-mail, as a common and traditional way to apply for a position, is still highly popular for its simplicity –no registration is needed. In addition, an application form is also available.

Your inbox is a treasure trove of promising job seekers.

Data validation

Lensa assists the application process with an extremely easy-to-use data validation step. This sends immediate feedback whenever a new application arrives, and improves the reliability of automatic compliance assessments.

In HR, precision and accuracy are a must.

Candidate feedback options

Lensa features various message templates and a wide range of fields to personalize form letters to make online correspondence faster. Candidates waiting for a response can be filtered by a number of parameters, and the system sends alerts if it detects a missing feedback at the end of the hiring process.
